What is a Group?

When you create an Ad, you will place it into a specific Group. The relationship between Ad & Group is similar to the relationship beteen Entry & Section. It's not possible for an ad to exist in more than one group.

You can generally think of an ad group as being in a "position". (ie: "Header Banner", "Right Sidebar")
